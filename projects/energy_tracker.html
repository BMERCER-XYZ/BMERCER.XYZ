Mobile Energy Consumption Monitor & Tracker
$ version 1.0.0 | status: ACTIVE DEVELOPMENT_
Project Overview
Platform
Cross-platform mobile application
Primary Function
Real-time energy consumption tracking and logging
Development Stack
SwiftUI | IOS | WatchOS

Simple Energy Tracker is an IOS & WatchOS application designed to monitor, display, and log energy consumption data. It was designed with the goal of making caloric tracking as simple as possible

Privacy Policy

Privacy and data security were carefully considered in the creation of Simple Energy Tracker.

Data Collection

Data collection within Simple Energy Tracker is performed with the user's explicit consent. Energy tracking from the watchOS app is accomplished using the WatchConnectivity framework. As with all Apple frameworks, it's incredibly robust and secure. For more information on exactly how it works please follow this link: WatchConnectivity Documentation.

Data Storage & Security

User data generated or observed within Simple Energy Tracker is securely stored either on device or within iCloud via Apple HealthKit. For further information on how HealthKit is used please follow this link: HealthKit Documentation. Data sharing/ access between the IOS and WatchOS app is accomplished using App Groups. For more information on how App Groups work please follow this link: App Groups Documentation. I can assure you that I have zero interest in any of your data.

Third-Party Sharing

Simple Energy Tracker does not sell, store, trade, or share personal data with third parties.
