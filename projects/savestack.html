Privacy Policy

Privacy and data security were carefully considered in the creation of SaveStack.

Data Collection

SaveStack does not collect any personal data from its users.

Data Storage & Security

User data generated or observed within SaveStack is securely stored on device. Data sharing/ access between the IOS app and any accompanying homescreen widget/s is accomplished using App Groups. For more information on how App Groups work please follow this link: App Groups Documentation. I can assure you that I have zero interest in any of your data.

Third-Party Sharing

SaveStack does not sell, store, trade, or share personal data with third parties.
